All names of things that are defined are identifiers.
 An identifier consists of 255 significant characters letter
s, digits and the underscore character, from which the first 
must be an alphanumeric character, or an underscore.
 As you may expect, constants must be definable at compile-t
ime.
 However, Pascal was also designed so that it could be compi
led in 1 pass from top to bottom the reason being to make com
piling fast and simple.
